Crystal Collector

Instructions

A random number will be given at the start of the game.

By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game when total score equals random number, you lose the game if total score goes above random number.

When you click the crystal you can see the hidden number that each crystal have.

Crystals will have new values for every new game.
